What Is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a simple business model. You recommend products or services that people already want. When someone purchases through your unique affiliate link, you earn a commission from the company. You don’t need to create your own product. You don’t need to handle deliveries. You don’t need to provide customer support. Your main job is learning how to connect the right people with the right products.
